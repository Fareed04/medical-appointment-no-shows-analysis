### üìå Project Overview
Why do patients miss their scheduled appointments? This project analyzes over **110,000 medical records** from Brazil to identify the variables that influence "No-shows." We transition from descriptive analytics to **Binary Classification** to predict whether a patient will show up.



---

### üìä Key Research Questions
1. Does the **waiting time** between booking and the appointment increase no-shows?
2. Are patients with **Scholarships** (government assistance) more likely to miss appointments?
3. How effective are **SMS reminders** in reducing absenteeism?
4. Can we predict a "No-Show" with at least **70% accuracy**?
